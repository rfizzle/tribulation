Features

Everything Tribulation does to make your world progressively more dangerous.

Scaling Axes

Mob stats are boosted by three independent axes that combine multiplicatively per attribute. Each axis produces a factor that gets added to the mob's base stat via attribute modifiers.

Time (Player Level)

Your playtime accumulates ticks (default: 72,000 ticks = 1 hour per level). Max level is 250. Every mob spawned near you uses your level to compute a time factor per stat: min(level * rate, cap).

Distance from Spawn

Beyond startingDistance (default: 1000 blocks), every 300 blocks adds 0.1 to the position bonus. Caps at 1.5. Only applies to health, damage, armor, and toughness.

Height (Y-Level)

Deviation from Y=62 by 30 blocks adds 0.1. Supports both positive and negative height scaling. Caps at 0.5.

Distance and height are optionally excluded in non-Overworld dimensions. Time scaling applies everywhere.

Tier-Gated Abilities

At five tier thresholds, mobs unlock special behaviors beyond raw stat boosts. Each ability can be individually toggled.

Tier Level Abilities Unlocked
Tier 1 50 Zombie reinforcements, Creeper shorter fuse, Hoglin knockback resistance
Tier 2 100 Skeleton sword switch, Spider/Cave Spider web placing, Drowned trident upgrade, Piglin crossbow
Tier 3 150 Zombie door-breaking, Spider crop trampling, Wither Skeleton sprint, Zoglin fire resistance
Tier 4 200 Skeleton flame arrows, Husk Hunger II, Wither Skeleton fire aspect, Vindicator damage resistance
Tier 5 250 Zombie sprinting, Creeper charged chance (25%), Spider leap attack, Zombified Piglin aggro range

Zombie Variants

Zombies have a chance to spawn as special variants with distinct visual and mechanical differences.

Big Zombie

10% chance. Scaled up 1.3x in size with +10 bonus health and +2 bonus damage, but 0.7x movement speed. A lumbering tank.

Speed Zombie

10% chance. 1.3x movement speed but -10 health. Fast and fragile — ambush predator behavior.

Boss Scaling

Boss mobs (Ender Dragon, Wither, Elder Guardian, and any entity in the c:bosses tag) use a separate, gentler scaling formula.

Formula

Time factor: level * bossTimeFactor / maxLevel. Distance uses bossDistanceFactor. Height scaling is skipped entirely for bosses.

Defaults

Max factor: 3.0x. Time rate: 0.3. Distance rate: 0.1. Combined factor capped at bossMaxFactor. Works in all dimensions.

Death Relief

When you die, your player level decreases by 2 (configurable). This provides a natural difficulty rubber-band: dying frequently keeps your level in check, while surviving pushes the ceiling higher.

  • Default reduction: 2 levels per death
  • Cooldown: 6,000 ticks (5 minutes) between reductions
  • Will not reduce below minimumLevel (default: 0)
  • Can be disabled entirely

Shatter Shards

Rare item drops from scaled mobs that let you intentionally lower your difficulty level.

  • Drop chance: 0.5% per scaled mob kill (configurable)
  • Only drop when player is at level 25+ (configurable)
  • Consuming a shard reduces your level by 5
  • Applies mild debuffs: Slowness II, Mining Fatigue II, Weakness II for 10 seconds

Hardcore Hearts Opt-in

Disabled by default. When enabled, each death permanently reduces your maximum health.

  • Lose 2 half-hearts per death (configurable: 1–20)
  • Minimum health floor: 2 half-hearts (1 heart)
  • Restore hearts using Heart Fragment items (+2 half-hearts each)
  • Crafted from 4 Shatter Shards + 1 Golden Apple
  • Admin command to restore/reset hearts

Soul Inventory Opt-in

Disabled by default. When enabled, your inventory is destroyed on death unless items carry the Soulbound enchantment.

  • Enchantment ID: tribulation:soulbound
  • Configurable to use any external enchantment ID
  • Option to also destroy XP on death
  • Respects the vanilla keepInventory gamerule when configured
  • Forces meaningful gear decisions — you cannot protect everything

Modded Mob Support

Tribulation automatically scales any modded mob that extends Monster (hostile mob base class).

  • Applies zombie-equivalent health and damage scaling only (no speed/armor changes)
  • Can exclude entire mod namespaces via excludedNamespaces
  • Can be overridden with full-ID entries in the scaling map for fine-tuning
  • Toggled via unlistedHostileMobs.enabled

Extra XP & Loot

Scaled mobs reward more experience to compensate for their increased difficulty.

  • XP multiplied by the mob's scaling factor, up to 2.0x (configurable)
  • Optional extra loot drops (disabled by default): each killed mob has a chance to drop an additional copy of its loot table
